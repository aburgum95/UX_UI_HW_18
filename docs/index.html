Pura Vida

Pura Vida is your guide to a sustainable travel venture. This travel app gives you reliable reviews from the locals. Each place on the app has been vetted and serves the environment while also supporting the people working there. This prototype was created using Figma, Miro, and Invision.

Bin Scout

Using Figma prototyping, Bin Scout was designed to facilitate the recycling process by providing users with a way to locate nearby recycling centers, track what and how much they recycle, as well as actively engage with their community to make our world greener. During the research phase, the team dove into an extensive user analysis using google forms, video interviews, and usability testing, along with compiling the data using Miro. e

Energy.Gov

The redesign of Energy.gov focused on fixing problems identified during heuristic evaluations and user testing. The new website encompasses an easy-to-use navigation bar and an enhanced landing page. The design utilizes animations created in Figma and Adobe XD programs and includes a sleek new design with refreshed UI and a cohesive visual vocabulary.
